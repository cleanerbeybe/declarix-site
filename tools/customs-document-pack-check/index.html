DIRECT ANSWER

What belongs in a customs document handoff?

A strong handoff gives the reviewer the source pack, party roles, goods and origin evidence, valuation inputs, procedure decisions, controls, and a named owner for every open question. The result is not more paperwork. It is fewer avoidable stops once preparation begins.

HOW TO USE IT

Map it. Rank it. Hand it over.

Tick a row when the evidence, decision, or responsible owner is mapped for the handoff. Leave it open when the point still needs work. Select “Review & prioritise” to turn the open rows into a P1–P3 action list for the desk.

Your individual selections stay in this page and disappear when it is closed or refreshed. The checker asks for no names, EORIs, values, goods descriptions, uploads, or free text.
