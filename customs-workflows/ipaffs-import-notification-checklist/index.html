DIRECT ANSWER · IPAFFS

Identify the commodity and risk route first, select an eligible border control post, submit the notification with its documents on time, then carry the reference into the customs and arrival pack.

FIVE-CHECK HANDOFF

Move the job in order.

Each checkpoint produces evidence for the next person or system. A failed checkpoint stays visible.

  1. 01
    Classify the control route

    Identify the product family, origin and current risk category before choosing the notification path.

  2. 02
    Check place and time

    Confirm an eligible border control post and the source-specific pre-notification deadline.

  3. 03
    Assemble health evidence

    Match certificates, commercial documents, commodity lines and transport details.

  4. 04
    Submit the IPAFFS notification

    Upload the required documents and retain the notification reference and version.

  5. 05
    Join the arrival handoff

    Keep IPAFFS, customs declaration, certificates and BCP instructions aligned through arrival.

HAVE READY

Inputs

  • Commodity and risk category
  • Origin and destination
  • Border control post
  • Health certificates
  • Arrival date and transport
STOP AND RESOLVE

Do not hand off green

  • Commodity scope is unresolved
  • BCP does not handle the commodity
  • Certificate and consignment differ
  • Notification deadline is at risk
