Keep the transit open only as long as the movement is.

A T1 movement joins the principal, guarantee, NCTS declaration, transport documents and offices of departure and destination. The handoff is not complete until destination evidence closes the movement.

DIRECT ANSWER · T1 / NCTS

Name the principal and guarantee, obtain the NCTS MRN and accompanying document, present the goods at departure and destination, and retain discharge evidence until the guarantee is released.

FIVE-CHECK HANDOFF

Move the job in order.

Each checkpoint produces evidence for the next person or system. A failed checkpoint stays visible.

  1. 01
    Set the transit responsibility

    Confirm the principal, offices, route and guarantee before lodging the declaration.

  2. 02
    Lodge in NCTS

    Submit complete movement and guarantee data and resolve rejection before presentation.

  3. 03
    Release at departure

    Present goods and documents; retain the MRN/TAD and any seal or itinerary controls.

  4. 04
    Carry the movement evidence

    Keep goods, transport identifiers and accompanying record aligned through offices of transit.

  5. 05
    Present and close at destination

    Obtain arrival/control results and discharge evidence; investigate movements that remain open.

HAVE READY

Inputs

  • Principal and EORI
  • Guarantee reference/access
  • Offices and route
  • Goods and transport data
  • MRN/TAD
STOP AND RESOLVE

Do not hand off green

  • Guarantee validation fails
  • Goods differ from declaration
  • MRN/TAD is unavailable
  • Destination has not discharged the movement
