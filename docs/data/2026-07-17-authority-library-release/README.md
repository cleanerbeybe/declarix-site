# Authority library release

Date: 2026-07-17

## Outcome

This release adds 13 indexable, source-led acquisition routes and 13 ungated downloadable assets:

- one four-question Incoterms chooser;
- five term sheets for FOB, CIF, DAP, FCA, and DDP;
- one source-faithful explorer of HMRC Customs Administrative Burden Wave 2;
- six operator workflows for GMR/GVMS, PVA, IPAFFS, ENS/ICS2, DDA, and T1/NCTS;
- five Incoterms SVG maps, six workflow SVG diagrams, one aggregate CSV, and one press chart.

Every route moves from a useful answer into a related browser workpaper or calculator and then a
numbers-call CTA. Downloads are ungated. No email, document, identifier, or free-text field was added.

## Why this order

1. The Incoterms chooser creates a broad commercial-intent hub and distributes internal authority to
   high-demand term pages.
2. Term sheets bridge directly into the customs-value and duty workpaper, joining informational search
   to an existing bottom-of-funnel task.
3. HMRC burden data creates a quotable, linkable research asset and bridges to the desk-cost calculator.
4. Six workflow diagrams expand topical coverage around the operational jobs that create entry evidence.
5. Every asset links to primary sources, a related task, and a conversion path without leading with
   governance copy.

## Frozen demand evidence

The first four approved seed terms were FOB, CIF, DAP, and FCA. DDP was selected as the fifth from the
frozen GB keyword export at `cleanerbeybe/declarix/SEO/2026-07-03/keywords.csv`: 1,400 monthly volume,
KD 12, and 741 available clicks. No current Ahrefs subscription or invented refresh was used.

## Publication model

The generator fails closed on duplicate paths, titles, and H1s; source count; sitemap and LLM-file
membership; orphan pages; downloadable-asset drift; structured data; prohibited FAQ/HowTo schema;
privacy-safe analytics; and accidental form collection. The routes remain static apart from the
Incoterms chooser and event emission.
