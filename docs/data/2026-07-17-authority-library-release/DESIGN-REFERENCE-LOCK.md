# Design reference lock

Date: 2026-07-17

The reference is the live Declarix homepage and existing report/workpaper system at getdeclarix.com.

Locked characteristics:

- Archivo display and body typography with IBM Plex Mono labels;
- deep teal ink, warm off-white paper, amber navigation/breadcrumb fields, and green reserved for action;
- one-pixel rules, square geometry, asymmetric hero/ledger composition, and dense accessible tables;
- direct answer, operator map, downloadable evidence, related tool, and numbers-call progression;
- responsive stacking without horizontal page overflow.

Rejected directions:

- rounded-card grids and pill-heavy SaaS layouts;
- indigo or purple replacement palettes;
- serif editorial redesigns that lose the existing commercial force;
- decorative dashboards without a real source, task, download, or next action;
- defensive legal copy in the hero or conversion slot.

The one P1 found during visual QA was ledger-stamp overflow for long labels. The stamp scale was reduced
globally and rechecked on `5 TERMS`, `460 VOICES`, and `GMR / GVMS` panels.
