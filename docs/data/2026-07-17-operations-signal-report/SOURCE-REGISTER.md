## BIFA member directory

- Directory: <https://bifa.org/members/>

- Limitation: membership and service specialization establish sector relevance, not current buying timing.

- Directory: <https://fiata.org/directory/gb/>
- Limitation: membership can identify freight forwarders but does not prove that a UK office performs declarations in-house.

## Public website discovery

The source research made a limited, identifiable, robots-aware pass over the 854-company directory union:

- home page and at most one same-domain service/customs page;
- no more than two megabytes per page;
- no login, gated content, form submission, or contact enrichment;
- short evidence excerpts in the private source package only.

Website discovery terms routed manual research. They did not auto-qualify a company.
