### B2 · Visual calm (the "not clean enough" fix — density is the enemy, not the brand)

1. **Air budget doubles.** Section internal padding 34 → 64px desktop (24 → 40 mobile); minimum 120px between a box's last element and its bottom rule; hero gets 12vh more headroom.
2. **Ink hierarchy.** Inside dense components (grid, docket, ledger, cards) internal rules drop to **1px at 60% ink**; structural borders stay 1.5px full ink; the outer frame stays 2px. Three visible weights, consistently applied — density reads as order, not clutter.
3. **One mono caption per moment.** Audit every section: where 2–3 mono lines stack (hero has price cue + footnote; Box 3 has several), keep the single most valuable, fold or delete the rest. Hero keeps the price cue; the 3×-modelling footnote moves into the docket's assumption line.
4. **Copy trim, 20% target.** Body paragraphs max 3 lines at desktop width. Known cuts: hero body loses "evidence pinned to every field" (the scene shows it 400px later); Box 5 body loses the second sentence; FAQ answers stay.
5. **Bigger quiet headlines.** Section heads 34 → 40px desktop; letter-spacing −0.02em. Fewer, larger, calmer.

### B4 · Animation de-jank (the "makeshift" fix — fewer, straighter, flawless)

1. **Straighten the tethers.** Replace curved beziers with **orthogonal routed connectors** (horizontal → 90° elbow → vertical, 1.5px, 2px corner radius exception permitted at elbows only). Curves read as scribble; routed lines read as engineering. Draw animation unchanged.
2. **One thing moves at a time.** Chip flights become strictly sequential (next launches when previous lands); scrub smoothing 0.4 → 0.55. Slower + singular = intentional.
3. **Fixed rest-angle set.** All rotated paper snaps to one of {−2.5°, −1°, 0°, +1.5°, +3°}. Random-looking but art-directed; eliminates the ransom-note effect.
4. **Kill list.** v2.4's C5 velocity tilt: dead. Any effect that misses 60fps on an iPhone 12: ships disabled, no exceptions. Marquee speed −20%.
5. **Micro-precision sweep:** all easings from the two tokens only; all durations from {120, 300, 450, 600}ms; chip landing flash exactly 120ms; stamp choreography untouched — it's the signature, protect it.
